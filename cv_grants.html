Academic CV

Funding, grants and prizes awarded

  • MSCA Postdoctoral Fellowships (€260,347.92), 2024
    Awarded a 2-year Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions European Fellowship in the Horizon Europe programme of the European Commission (call: HORIZON-MSCA-2024-PF-01-01) for the proposed project METrIC - Modelling of Epithelial-to-Mesenchymal Transition in Invading Cells
  • BOUM SMAI funding (€1000), 2023
    Financial support of the Société de Mathématiques Appliquées et Industrielles ("BOUge tes Mathématiques" project) for the organisation of the workshop Mathematical challenges in modelling cancer dynamics: from analysis to numerics and validation in Paris in October 2023.
  • PEPS JCJC funding (€4900), 2023
    Financial support awarded by Insmi within the programme Projet Exploratoire Premier Soutien «Jeunes Chercheurs et Jeunes Chercheuses» for the project Conservative numerical schemes for novel structured PDE models of cancer invasion in collaboration with Alexandre Poulain (Université de Lille).
  • UFR 929 funding (€1500), 2023
    Financial support of the Unité de Formation et de Recherche 929 (Faculté de Mathématiques) for the organisation of the workshop Mathematical challenges in modelling cancer dynamics: from analysis to numerics and validation in Paris in October 2023.
  • Junior Fellowship, 2022
    Fellowship for the participation to the workshop Parabolic and kinetic models in population dynamics in Toulouse in September 2022.
  • Paris Region Fellowship Programme laureate (€257,760), 2022
    Research Fellowship funded by the Paris Region and the European Union, under the Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions, with the proposed project on Mechanistic models of cell migration and cancer invasion: analysis, numerics, validation
  • IHP financial support (€4500), 2021
    Financial support awarded by the Institute Henri Poincaré for the participation to the Mathematical modeling of organization in living matter thematic program in Paris during 10/01-01/04 2022
  • SMBdevBio Poster Prize 1 ($250), 2020
    Poster Prize awarded by the Society for Mathematical Biology, Developmental Biology subgroup, at the online SMB2020 meeting
  • LMS ECR Travel Grant (£500), 2020

  • PhD funding (£49124.25), 2018
    Scholarship awarded by the School of Mathematics and Statistics, University of St Andrews
  • The Principal’s Scholarship for Academic Excellence (£1000), 2018

  • The Deans’ list, 2014 - 2018

  • Research scholarship (£1684.29), 2017
